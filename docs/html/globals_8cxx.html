◆ button_shader

Shader button_shader = {}

This shader is used internally by the Button class.

  • If the button is idle, then it's texture will be drawn with nothing else.
  • If the button is being hovered, it's texture will appear with a slight white tint and a white outline.
  • If the button is being pressed, it's texture will appear with a dark tint and a white outline.
